Azuli

Azuli Elurra is a member of team FOES.

Appearance

She is of average stature pale complexion with long blue hair and with a white steak on her right side like her sister, her eyes are of bright lapis. She's got a white t-shirt with a vest on top with her emblem on the vest, showing of her fishscaley arms. She got jeans shorts and white sneakers.

Personality

She's got a very possitive, happy, laid back personality and is very proud of her sister.

Backstory

When she was three she fell of a boat when out in a lake fishing, and because of her age, she couldn't swim. This made her unlock her semblance of water breathing. But her sister and mother didn't know this, they thought she was gone, so when she managed to get up to the surface after some time she swore not to scare them like that again.

Weapons & Combat

Her weapon is Aquatic Harpy. It is harpoon with a point big and mean looking enouth to pin down the biggest of grimm. It can also be used as a spear in close combat. It does not have any offensive dust capability,

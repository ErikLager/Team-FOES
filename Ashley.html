Ashley Nila is a member of team JAMN.

Appearance

She has dark hair and dark eyes, and usually wears a black hoodie.

Personality

She's got generally a possitive personallity, but isn't afraid of dark jokes sometimes. Sometimes she jokes about commiting crimes.

Weapons & Combat

Her weapons consist of a bullwhip and a boomerang.

Trivia

  • Mer mother is a human while her father is a wolf faunus.
  • She was inspired to use a boomerang from a kids show.

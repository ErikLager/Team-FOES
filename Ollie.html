Ollie Akijan

Ollie

Ollie Akijan is a member of team FOES.

Appearance

She's a bit taller than average with a slight tan complexion with Olive hair in a lazy mohawk, exposing the right side of her head. Her eyes are the same color as her hair. She wears a long-sleeved shirt with some vine-like patterns on it with a matching long skirt to make it easier on her bearded dragon tail. She also wears a neckless with a photo inside, however, this is often hidden under her shirt. The backside of the neckless is her emblem. A olive branch

Personality

She's got a possitive personality and always tries to keep the mood up, but she knows when to not try.

Backstory

When playing in the forever fall forest with her younger brother in her childhood, they were attacked by a grimm. It got her brother and she could only watch as it killed him. All her life she's been told to run away so she did, tears streaming down her face. But it seemed like she wasn't fast enough. A grimm versus a child, but this made her awaken her semblance, she could channel energy to her mussles and she managed to get away to sefety. The grimm was taken care of soon there after, but it didn't change the fact that her brother was gone. She swore not to lose anyone else to the grimm. So she enrolled at the huntsman accademy. When she moved to Haven she was meet with a harch reality, faunus kind wasn't welcome there. This resulted in constant bullying, mostly because of her tail, but also her eyes and hairstyle, being the same she always had. She talked to her parents about it but they recomended not to change it and the bullies were just immature. Later her parents got divorced it wasn't that big of a fight, but they couldn't agree on where to stay.

Weapons & Combat

She fights with two warfans named Shion and Momo Shion having fire dust integraded into it and Momo having ice dust in it.

Semblance

She can power up her mussles for a spike of strength. When she tries to arm wrestle someone she can activate her semblance and win in an instance, or lift a heavy object like a beam or a car to let someone escape.
